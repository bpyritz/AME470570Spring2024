Textiles

Mediums and Uses

I first got into textile work at age 15 when I fell in love with punk music. I saw people from the community making and sewing their own patches onto jackets and vests and I began to do the same. Since then, I have been printing designs, namely band logos, turning them into stencils, and painting them onto patch fabric. I then sew by hand onto the item of clothing I want it on. Sometimes I use a machine after hand sewing to make the patch extra secure. I've expanded to sewing on shirts and pants as well as jackets and vests. Since around the same age, I've also painted directly onto denim and leather jackets, creating custom designs related to punk and painting large pieces onto the clothes. In the past year, I have learned to heat transfer any design onto tshirts and pants, and have also recently begun screen printing.
